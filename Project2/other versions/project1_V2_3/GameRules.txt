Qualifier Rules:
1) You will have 6 questions followed one by another.
2) You will have 90 seconds to answer all the questions,
   if you fail to do so you will fail.
3) You need to get atleast 4 correct answers to qualify for the Main Event.

Main Event Rules:
This game is similar to Who wants to be a Millionaire.
A question and four options will be displayed.
Only one of them is the correct answer.
You have to enter the option you believe is correct.
A correct answer will move you to the next question while a wrong answer will end the game.
